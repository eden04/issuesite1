By providing awareness to campus life officials and students, Without Waste aspires to decrease harmful pratices and increase eco-friendly options. College students find recycling and sustainbility important, but it's not always provided within dorms,a round campus or in cafeterias. Without Waste IL seeks to explain the harmful effects of litttering and trash buildup, and why Illinois universities need to care about their campuses.

Reduce

Reduce pollution, food waste, and greenhouse gas emissions to sustain the environment.

Reuse

Reuse bottles, shopping, and holiday decorations to diminish all waste.

Recycle

Recycle glass, plastic, and paper to impact Earth's nature and people.

As every American student has once learned, the core foundations of the climate crisis are reduce, reuse, and recycle. Whether you suffer through the effects of climate change or not, Illinois residents have a right to sustainability.

College students, no matter their distance from home, should recycle on campus. As the next generation rises, it is essential to promote waste management and responsible recycling. A single person can help make a cleaner, safer world.
